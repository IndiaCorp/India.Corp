About India Corp

India Corp is a proud Indian enterprise dedicated to promoting two of our nation's finest offerings - premium Indian coffee and high-quality national flags.

Founded in 2010, we combine traditional values with modern quality standards to bring you authentic products that celebrate India's rich heritage and agricultural excellence.

Our mission is to support Indian farmers and manufacturers while providing customers with the very best of Indian products.

Our Indian Coffee Selection

We source our coffee directly from plantations across Southern India, ensuring the highest quality while supporting local farming communities.

Karnataka Arabica

Grown in the lush Western Ghats, our Arabica beans are known for their mild flavor and delightful aroma. These medium-roast beans produce a well-balanced cup with subtle fruity notes.

Kerala Robusta

Our robust Robusta blend comes from the fertile hills of Kerala. With higher caffeine content and a stronger, earthier flavor, it's perfect for those who prefer a bold coffee experience.

Chikmagalur Special

Often called the 'Coffee Land of Karnataka', Chikmagalur produces some of India's finest coffee. Our special blend combines the best beans from this region for an exceptional taste.

Our Indian Flag Collection

We manufacture and distribute high-quality Indian national flags made with pride in India, using materials that withstand weather conditions while maintaining vibrant colors.

Standard National Flags

Available in various sizes from small desk flags to large outdoor flags. Made from durable polyester that resists fading and tearing, perfect for everyday display.

Premium Khadi Flags

Authentic hand-spun khadi cotton flags as per Bureau of Indian Standards. These flags are specially treated to maintain color fastness and durability.

Specialty Flags

Includes car flag sets, ceremonial flags, and institutional flags for schools and government buildings. All made with the same attention to quality and respect for our national symbol.
