About India Corp

Founded in 2010, India Corp is dedicated to promoting two of India's finest products - our world-class coffee and our national pride.

We source our coffee directly from plantations in Karnataka and Kerala, ensuring fair prices for farmers and premium quality for our customers. Our flags are manufactured with the highest quality materials, made entirely in India.

By choosing India Corp, you support Indian farmers, artisans, and the Make in India initiative.
